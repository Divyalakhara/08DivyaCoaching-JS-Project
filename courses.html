Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tuition programs

Our curriculum covers all major school subjects:

Primary (1-5): Math, English, EVS, Hindi, Computer Basics
Middle (6-8): Math, Science (Physics/Chemistry/Biology), Social Studies, English, Hindi/Sanskrit
High (9-12): Science subjects with specialization for board exams

We also offer special classes for competitive exams like Olympiads, NTSE, etc.

We maintain an optimal teacher-student ratio for maximum engagement:

Primary classes: 1 teacher per 4 students
Middle classes: 1 teacher per 5 students
High school: 1 teacher per 6 students

This ensures individual attention while allowing peer learning opportunities.

We have a comprehensive progress tracking system:

- Weekly quizzes and assignments
- Monthly comprehensive tests
- Term-wise mock exams (for higher classes)
- Individual performance dashboards
- Regular parent-teacher meetings

The results help customize future learning plans.
