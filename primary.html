Grade-Specific Programs

Tailored programs designed to meet the unique needs of each grade level.

Grade 1 Foundations Program

Our Grade 1 program focuses on building strong literacy, numeracy, and life skills to ensure a solid academic foundation for future success.

English (Reading & Writing)

  • ✅ Phonemic awareness and phonics instruction
  • ✅ Sight word recognition and vocabulary building
  • ✅ Reading comprehension with short stories
  • ✅ Basic grammar: nouns, verbs, opposites, prepositions
  • ✅ Writing simple sentences and short paragraphs

Mathematics

  • ✅ Number sense & counting 1–120
  • ✅ Addition & subtraction within 20
  • ✅ Place value: tens & ones
  • ✅ Introduction to shapes & patterns
  • ✅ Time (hours/half-hours), money & measurements

Environmental Studies (EVS)

  • ✅ Myself & My Family
  • ✅ Food, Water & Shelter
  • ✅ Animals, Birds & Plants
  • ✅ Seasons, Weather & Safety Rules
  • ✅ Community helpers & cleanliness

Hindi / Regional Language

  • ✅ Varnmala (alphabets) & matras
  • ✅ Two & three-letter words
  • ✅ Rhymes & short stories

Art, Craft & Life Skills

  • ✅ Drawing, coloring & basic craft
  • ✅ Good manners, sharing & teamwork
  • ✅ Physical activities & motor skill development

Grade 2 Building Blocks Program

Designed to help students master more complex concepts in reading, writing, mathematics, and life skills.

English (Reading & Writing)

  • ✅ Reading fluency and expression development
  • ✅ Comprehension for fiction and nonfiction
  • ✅ Paragraph writing and simple narratives
  • ✅ Basic grammar: nouns, pronouns, adjectives, tenses
  • ✅ Informal letter writing & short essays

Mathematics

  • ✅ Understanding place value up to 500
  • ✅ Addition & subtraction (2-3 digit, regrouping)
  • ✅ Multiplication tables 2–10
  • ✅ Division (sharing & grouping)
  • ✅ Time (hours, half-hours, quarter-hours) & money
  • ✅ Measurement (length, weight, capacity)
  • ✅ 2D & 3D shapes, patterns & simple data handling

Environmental Studies (EVS)

  • ✅ Family, neighborhood & community helpers
  • ✅ Food habits, hygiene & healthy living
  • ✅ Plants, animals & natural resources
  • ✅ Seasons, festivals & weather
  • ✅ Transport, communication & safety rules

Hindi / Regional Language

  • ✅ Varnmala revision & advanced matras
  • ✅ 4–5 letter words & sentence formation
  • ✅ Short stories, poems & basic grammar

Art, Craft & Life Skills

  • ✅ Drawing with details, patterns & coloring
  • ✅ Paper craft & creative projects
  • ✅ Good manners, empathy & teamwork
  • ✅ Physical activities & coordination games

Grade 3 Mastery Program

A comprehensive program aimed at solidifying reading, writing, math, and life skills for third graders.

English (Reading & Writing)

  • ✅ Analyzing character development and plot structure
  • ✅ Developing comprehension skills for various genres
  • ✅ Writing multi-paragraph essays with clear structure
  • ✅ Grammar: nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, tenses
  • ✅ Informal and simple formal letter writing, story writing

Mathematics

  • ✅ Numbers up to 1000, place value & expanded form
  • ✅ Addition & subtraction (3–4 digit numbers with regrouping)
  • ✅ Mastering multiplication & division facts (tables up to 12)
  • ✅ Understanding fractions (half, quarter, comparisons)
  • ✅ Measurement: length, weight, capacity (standard units)
  • ✅ Time (12-hour & 24-hour clock) & money transactions
  • ✅ Data interpretation & introduction to bar graphs
  • ✅ Basic geometry: angles, lines, perimeter & area

Environmental Studies (EVS)

  • ✅ Food chain, healthy diet & natural resources
  • ✅ Plants, animals & their habitats
  • ✅ Water cycle, air, soil & conservation
  • ✅ Family, community roles & safety rules
  • ✅ Seasons, weather patterns & basic map reading

Hindi / Regional Language

  • ✅ Grammar: gender, number, synonyms, antonyms
  • ✅ Short essays, poems & picture description
  • ✅ Sentence formation & story writing

Science

  • ✅ Living & non-living things
  • ✅ Human body basics & sense organs
  • ✅ States of matter: solid, liquid, gas
  • ✅ Light, sound & simple force concepts

Art, Craft & Life Skills

  • ✅ Perspective drawing, shading & patterns
  • ✅ Creative projects & craftwork
  • ✅ Problem solving, teamwork & leadership

Grade 4 Advanced Program

A program designed to challenge fourth graders and enhance their critical thinking, problem solving, and academic skills.

English (Reading & Writing)

  • ✅ Evaluating themes and main ideas in texts
  • ✅ Writing persuasive essays and opinion pieces
  • ✅ Analyzing informational texts and media
  • ✅ Grammar: nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, tenses
  • ✅ Letter writing (formal & informal), diary entries, story writing
  • ✅ Vocabulary building: synonyms, antonyms, homonyms

Mathematics

  • ✅ Solving multi-step word problems
  • ✅ Numbers up to 10,000 & Roman numerals
  • ✅ Multi-digit multiplication & division
  • ✅ Understanding decimals and their operations
  • ✅ Fractions: equivalent fractions, addition & subtraction
  • ✅ Measurement: length, weight, capacity (unit conversion)
  • ✅ Time (elapsed time) & money transactions
  • ✅ Introduction to angles, perimeter, area & symmetry
  • ✅ Data handling: bar graphs, pictographs, tally marks

Environmental Studies (EVS)

  • ✅ Plant life, photosynthesis & animal adaptations
  • ✅ Human body systems (digestive & respiratory)
  • ✅ Natural resources, conservation & pollution awareness
  • ✅ Weather, climate & map skills (directions & scale)
  • ✅ Transport & communication history

Science

  • ✅ States of matter: solid, liquid, gas
  • ✅ Force, motion & energy (basic concepts)
  • ✅ Light, shadows & reflections
  • ✅ Introduction to Earth & the Solar System

Hindi / Regional Language

  • ✅ Advanced grammar: Sandhi, Samas (intro)
  • ✅ Story & essay writing, poems
  • ✅ Picture composition & sentence structure

Art, Craft & Life Skills

  • ✅ Poster making, perspective drawing, shading
  • ✅ Origami & 3D craft projects
  • ✅ Leadership, decision making & teamwork activities

Grade 5 Leadership Program

A program designed to prepare fifth graders for middle school with advanced skills in reading, writing, math, and life skills.

Reading & Writing Focus

  • ✅ Analyzing complex texts and themes
  • ✅ Writing research papers and reports
  • ✅ Developing persuasive and narrative writing skills
  • ✅ Grammar: all parts of speech, tenses, direct & indirect speech
  • ✅ Active & Passive Voice (introduction)
  • ✅ Letter writing, diary entries, article & report writing
  • ✅ Vocabulary: idioms, synonyms, antonyms, phrases

Math Focus

  • ✅ Mastering fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • ✅ Large number operations & place value up to 1,00,000
  • ✅ Introduction to algebraic concepts
  • ✅ Geometry: triangles, quadrilaterals, perimeter & area
  • ✅ Measurement conversions & real-world applications
  • ✅ Time, money, profit & loss, simple interest (intro)
  • ✅ Data handling: bar graphs, line graphs, pictographs
  • ✅ Number patterns & basic algebraic expressions

Science

  • ✅ Plant & animal adaptations
  • ✅ Human body systems: circulatory, nervous, digestive
  • ✅ Matter: physical & chemical changes
  • ✅ Force & motion, simple machines
  • ✅ Energy: renewable & non-renewable sources
  • ✅ Earth, layers, Solar System & phases of the Moon
  • ✅ Weather, climate & global warming

Social Studies / EVS

  • ✅ Ancient civilizations & Indian history basics
  • ✅ Geography: maps, latitude, longitude & continents
  • ✅ Civics: rights, duties, democracy & government
  • ✅ Culture & heritage studies

Hindi / Regional Language

  • ✅ Advanced grammar: Sandhi, Samas, Alankar basics
  • ✅ Essays, letters & story writing
  • ✅ Poems & comprehension
  • ✅ Vocabulary building & sentence structure

Art, Craft & Life Skills

  • ✅ Perspective drawing & landscape art
  • ✅ 3D craft, models & poster design
  • ✅ Teamwork, leadership & problem-solving activities
